Installation
============
Unzip into your Quake folder.


Running the game
================
Normal Quake:
1.) Open the 'bat' folder
2.) Double-click on winquake.bat


Important Notes
===============
- You should have wordwrap enabled in your text editor to view this file.
- Refer to fbra.txt and readme013test.txt / fbca085.txt / fbrarena013se.txt
for more information on the Frogbot.
